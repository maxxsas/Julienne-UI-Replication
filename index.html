Blueberry Baked Oats

By

@christianna.nutritionist

•

Original Recipe Link

When you want cake for breakfast but you still want it to be nutritious, make my blueberry baked oats. Delicious, easy and great for a weekend breakfast or for meal prep. I serve it with Greek yoghurt for extra protein and it keeps me full and satisfied for hours!

Ingredients

Apple Puree

10.5 oz

Eggs Or Flax Eggs

2 large

Vanilla Extract

2 Tsp

Oat Milk

1 3/4 Cups

Maple Syrup Or Honey

1/4 Cups

Olive Oil

2 Tbsp

Jumbo Oats

3 1/4 Cups

Ground Cinnamon

1½ tsp

Baking Powder

1 Tsp

Fine Salt

¼ tsp

Fresh Blueberries

7 oz

Greek Yoghut

Walnuts

Honey

Cooking Steps
01
Preheat the oven to 180°C.
02
In an oven-safe baking dish, mix the apple puree, eggs or flax eggs, vanilla, oat milk, honey, and olive oil. Mix to combine well.

Apple Puree

10.5 oz

Eggs Or Flax Eggs

Vanilla Extract

Oat Milk

03
Add the oats, cinnamon, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.

Jumbo Oats

3 1/4 Cups

Ground Cinnamon

1½ tsp

Baking Powder

1 Tsp

Fine Salt

¼ tsp

04
Add the blueberries and gently mix, making sure to keep a few for the top - it just looks pretty.

Fresh Blueberries

7 oz

05
Bake for around 35 minutes till the top is golden brown and it’s just set.
06
Serve with Greek yoghurt, walnuts, and a little extra honey.
